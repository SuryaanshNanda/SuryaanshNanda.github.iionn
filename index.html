INTERACTIVE FLOWER

Everlasting flower for your everlasting love. A perfect not-only Valentine's day gift to your loved one. This mechanical flower will bloom into any color possible with just gentle touch of your hand. Its six petals will slowly open and light up. When petals are closed they create incredible ambient light with leaf patterns.

This flower hides 7 neopixel rgb leds,each petal has 6 smd white leds. Petals are opened by a push rod which is going all the way down the stem to the base.The push rod is controlled by a movement of a small hobby servo.
